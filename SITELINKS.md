## How to Add Sitelinks in Google Ads

### Step 1: Go to Your Campaign
1. Log in to Google Ads
2. Select your campaign
3. Click "Ads & extensions" â†’ "Extensions"

### Step 2: Create Sitelink Extensions
1. Click the blue "+" button
2. Select "Sitelink extension"
3. Add the following:

**Sitelink 1:**
- Sitelink text: `24/7 Emergency Service`
- Description line 1: `Fast response for urgent issues`
- Description line 2: `Available around the clock`
- Final URL: `https://yourdomain.com/emergency-service.html`

**Sitelink 2:**
- Sitelink text: `Get Free Quote`
- Description line 1: `No obligation estimate`
- Description line 2: `Professional service guaranteed`
- Final URL: `https://yourdomain.com/free-quote.html`

### Step 3: Additional Recommended Sitelinks

You can add up to 4 sitelinks. Here are suggestions for the main page sections:

## Best Practices

### Sitelink Strategy:
1. **Emergency Service** - Captures high-intent, urgent searches
2. **Free Quote** - Lower funnel, information gathering
3. **Services** - Mid-funnel, browsing options
4. **Contact** - Direct action for ready customers

### Tips:
- Use action words (Get, Call, Request, View)
- Keep descriptions under 35 characters
- Test different combinations
- Monitor which sitelinks get the most clicks
- Use mobile-preferred sitelinks for phone calls

### Tracking:
- Each sitelink page has the same form submission tracking
- Monitor conversions by landing page in Google Analytics
- Track which sitelinks drive the most leads
